What is the checkers?

  • Place the pieces on every other dark square, creating a staggered pattern by rows as depicted on the board
  • Each player has pieces of a different color

How to play checkers

  • Players can only move their pieces diagonally forward (towards their opponent)
  • Regular pieces can only move one square at a time diagonally
  • When a regular piece reaches the opponent's back row, it is "kinged" and gets a crown
    • Kings can move both forward and backward diagonally
  • Capture is mandatory when an opponent's piece is diagonally adjacent and there is an empty square immediately beyond it
  • The game ends when one player captures all of the opponent's pieces or blocks them from making a legal move
  • Win condition
    • Remove all your opponent's pieces from the board
    • Stop your opponents from being able to move any of their pieces
